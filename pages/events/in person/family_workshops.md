## Overview 
The family workshops during the year provided entertainment and learning opportunities for families with children too young to participate in after school programs. Many of them were run by the XR Star teen student interns.

***

## General Organization
The family workshops each had a general theme with about three or four stations based on that theme. While the workshops were open, families were free to enter and visit any of the different stations freely; they could stay for as long as they wanted. There were also some fun activities for smaller children that we had for every workshop such as marbles and building blocks.
